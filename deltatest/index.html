Data Acquisition & System Visibility

We provide complete data acquisition handling and operational visibility solutions. Our systems are designed to bolt onto your existing infrastructure, enabling real‑time insight without requiring a full system replacement.

  • Field data collection and aggregation
  • Live dashboards and operational analytics
  • Remote device monitoring and configuration
  • Integration with existing PLC, DCS, or historian systems
Do you offer hardware that can collect and transmit industrial data securely?

Field Node Technology (OmniBus™)

Fortiview develops next‑generation Field Node Data Acquisition Modules for industrial environments. Our current device, OmniBus™, is a universal HART communication module featuring:

  • Bluetooth connectivity
  • iOS app integration
  • Standalone field operation
  • Vendor‑agnostic HART communication
  • Expected release: Q4 2026
Do you support industrial automation or instrumentation in the field?

Industrial Automation & Instrumentation Support

Fortiview Holdings provides full‑spectrum industrial automation support and instrumentation technical services across all major brands and platforms.

  • Calibration, configuration, and troubleshooting of industrial instrumentation
  • Pneumatic, digital, and smart devices for flow, level, temperature, and analytical measurement
  • Advanced instrumentation support including Coriolis, magnetic flow, ultrasonic, radar, and guided‑wave radar transmitters across all major brands
  • HART communication, device diagnostics, and smart transmitter support
  • PLC, and DCS troubleshooting, logic development, and system optimization
  • Startup, commissioning, loop verification, and integration across all major OEM platforms

Whether you need digital infrastructure, business systems, field hardware, or industrial automation support, Fortiview Holdings provides solutions that scale with your operation.
